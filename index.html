About the Fund

Our LGBT Fund was established by Shelley Padnos and Carol Sarosik, a West Michigan couple who chose to spearhead changes in the community after attending OutGiving, a LGBT philanthropic conference sponsored by the Gill Foundation. Inspired by the conference and the insight that the LGBT community needs to initiate support for LGBT issues, they partnered with the Grand Rapids Community Foundation to lead the charge against issues faced by LGBT youth.
